[Icon: Sword]

Swordmaster

Key Characteristics:

Unleash a whirlwind of steel as a Swordmaster, an elite warrior specializing in the art of melee combat. Wielding blades with deadly precision and protected by personal shields, you are the storm on the battlefield. Your unmatched agility and devastating attacks make you a formidable force in any direct confrontation.

Works Best With:

Mentat, Bene Gesserit Adept

[Icon: Brain]

Mentat

Key Characteristics:

Become the living computer, the Mentat, whose mind is a fortress of logic and strategy. Process vast amounts of information, predict enemy maneuvers with chilling accuracy, and devise master plans that outwit any foe. While not a frontline combatant, your intellect is the sharpest weapon in your arsenal.

Works Best With:

Swordmaster, Fremen Guerrilla

[Icon: Eye]

Bene Gesserit Adept

Key Characteristics:

Step into the shadows as a Bene Gesserit Adept, a member of the enigmatic sisterhood. Master the power of 'The Voice' to command and control, perceive truths hidden to others, and subtly weave the threads of destiny. Your influence can turn the tide of conflict without drawing a single blade.

Works Best With:

Swordmaster, Mentat

[Icon: Crysknife]

Fremen Guerrilla

Key Characteristics:

Embrace the spirit of Arrakis as a Fremen Guerrilla, a hardened survivor and master of desert warfare. Move unseen through the dunes, strike with the deadly crysknife, and harness the harsh environment as your ally. Your resilience and cunning make you a phantom in the sand, an unexpected terror to your enemies.

Works Best With:

Mentat, other Fremen Guerrillas
